Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

AptYou Services Pvt Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AptyRead mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address or phone number for authentication purposes (via Google OAuth or Phone OTP through Firebase).
  • Child's Learning Progress: We store information about lesson completion, scores, and learning progress to provide personalized education.
  • Audio Recordings: When your child practices pronunciation, audio recordings may be temporarily processed using Google Speech-to-Text API for feedback purposes. These recordings are not permanently stored.
  • Payment Information: When you purchase learning levels, payment information is processed through secure third-party payment processors. We do not store credit card information.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information for app functionality and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the AptyRead educational service
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts
  • Track learning progress and provide personalized lessons
  • Process payments for level purchases
  • Provide pronunciation feedback through speech recognition
  • Improve our app and educational content
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services

3. Children's Privacy

AptyRead is designed for children ages 4-10. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations
  • We only collect information necessary for educational purposes
  • We do not sell or share children's personal information with third parties for marketing
  • Parents have the right to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information
  • Our app does not contain advertisements
  • We do not allow children to share personal information with other users

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • We use Firebase for secure authentication and data storage
  • Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and backend services
  • Google Speech-to-Text API: For pronunciation practice and feedback
  • Bunny CDN: For delivering educational content (audio, video, images)
  • Payment Processors: For processing in-app purchases

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through our account deletion page.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection (though this may limit app functionality)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. International Users

AptyRead is primarily designed for users in India and GCC countries. If you access our app from other regions, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in India, where our servers are located.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, through in-app notifications.

10. Compliance

We are committed to complying with:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - United States
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 - India
  • Google Play Families Policy
